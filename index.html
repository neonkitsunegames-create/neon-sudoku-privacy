Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

NeonSudoku is an offline Sudoku game developed by NeonKitsuneGames.

Data Collection

This application does not collect, store, process, or share any personal data.

The game does not require user registration and does not access contacts, location, photos, camera, microphone, or other personal information.

Internet Access

The application is designed to work offline and does not require an internet connection.

Third-Party Services

This application does not use advertising services, analytics services, or third-party tracking tools.

Local Data

The game may store game progress and settings locally on the user's device. This data stays on the device and is not transmitted to the developer or any third party.

Children's Privacy

This application does not knowingly collect any personal information from children or any other users.
