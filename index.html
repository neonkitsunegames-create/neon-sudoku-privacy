Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

NeonSudoku is a Sudoku game developed by Neon Kitsune Games.

Data Collection

NeonSudoku does not require user registration and does not ask users to provide their name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or precise location.

We do not operate our own user accounts, servers, or databases for storing personal user profiles.

However, the application uses third-party advertising services provided by Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads. These services may automatically collect, process, and share certain information, such as device information, advertising identifiers, IP address, diagnostic information, app interactions, ad interactions, and other technical data. This data may be used for advertising, analytics, fraud prevention, security, and service improvement.

Advertising

NeonSudoku displays advertisements using Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads. The application may show rewarded advertisements. Rewarded advertisements are optional: users may choose to watch an advertisement in order to receive an in-game benefit, such as continuing the game after defeat.

Advertising services may use device identifiers and other technical data to provide, measure, limit, and improve advertisements, and to help prevent fraud and abuse.

You can learn more about how Google uses information from apps that use Google services here:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Internet Access

The core Sudoku gameplay can work without an internet connection. However, an internet connection may be required to load and display advertisements.

Third-Party Services

The application uses the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads — used to display advertisements.

Third-party services may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies and terms.

Local Data

The game may store game progress, notes, difficulty settings, and other game settings locally on the user's device. This local game data is used to allow the user to continue gameplay and manage in-game settings.

Local game progress and settings are not sent to Neon Kitsune Games.

Children's Privacy

NeonSudoku does not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. The application does not require users to create an account or submit personal information.

Because the application uses third-party advertising services, those services may process certain technical and advertising-related data as described in their own policies.

User Choices

Users may manage advertising preferences through their device settings, including resetting or deleting the advertising ID where supported by the operating system.

If consent is required in a user's region, the application or the advertising provider may ask the user to make privacy or advertising choices.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: neonkitsunegames@gmail.com
